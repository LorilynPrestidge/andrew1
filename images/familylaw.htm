Family Law

Recognizing that issues involving family matters can be emotional and, at times, adversarial, we provide exceptionally personal service to each client, and work efficiently to resolve each case in a civil manner. Our legal services include divorce, spousal maintenance, child custody and support. We also assist with paternity, adoption, pre-nuptial agreements, and orders of protection.
